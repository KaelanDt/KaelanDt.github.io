Bonjour

I'm Kaelan Donatella, and welcome to my personal website!

I am a researcher in physics-based computing. Let me give a bit of background on that: I was trained in physics (because isn't it just the best field to study at university?), and worked at the intersection of quantum physics and machine learning during my PhD. I then realized that quantum computers were a bit too far ahead to continue working on it, and I stumbled upon Normal Computing, which I joined to pioneer new hardware, coined thermodynamic hardware.

You can find my publications here, some random thoughts and posts here, and a list of books that I started doing circa July 2024 here.
